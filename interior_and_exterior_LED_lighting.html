The lighting industry has undergone major changes in the past few years as homeowners have become more discerning and technology has grown. Traditional lighting suppliers just don't understand the newer technologies coming out and how to properly implement them. This often causes consumer frustration. The traditional incandescent/halogen lighting that has served us for years is going away due to inefficiency. Currently, that leaves us with either CFL (Compact Fluorescent Light} or LED (Light Emitting Diodes) technologies. We chose to back LED technology 67.

Why We Have Chosen to Devote Our Resources to LEDs 50

  • Electric lighting burns up to 25% of the average home energy budget. LEDs are currently the most efficient mainstream lighting source available typically using 1/30th the electricity that traditional bulbs use.42
  • LEDs combine the warmth of incandescent light with even better energy efficiency and lifespan than CFL boasts.
  • LEDs turn on instantly and don't contain mercury or other toxins that CFL has.
  • LEDs are more stable and give off less heat than both CFL and incandescent lights.
  • LEDs are more durable and emit no harmful UV light that can damage fine art, rugs or other sensitive materials.
