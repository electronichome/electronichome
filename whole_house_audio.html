Autoformers or Speaker Selectors

Connecting multiple speakers together for any multiroom audio system requires using either multiple channels of amplification or some form of impedance matching. Most amplifiers can only handle 1-2 pairs of speakers. There are two basic alternatives to protect an amplifier connected to multiple speakers. One is to use large automatically adjusting transformers (autoformers) that change the power draw of the secondary windings while maintaining a constant load on the primaries. Autoformers offer complete sound isolation from room to room, but can limit bass response 43. Autoformer mechanical controls allow clients to adjust the volume individually in each room 62. Most single source and even some multisource systems use autoformer. If a room's volume is at a loud setting and then turned off, it remains at that same setting when the zone is turned back on. For example, if someone entertains the night before and forgets to turn down the music, they now risk waking the whole house if they want to listen to the morning news program in the bathroom. To avoid this normally, one would have to go to each volume control and turn them off before turning the zone back on.

The other alternative is to use resistive devices such as speaker selectors 38. Speaker selectors waste anywhere from 20 - 50% of the amplifier's power and disrupt other rooms (if one turns the volume up in 1 room, all the others will turn down). Because of this we don’t recommend or use speaker selectors, and strictly work with autoformers.

Meridian Multi-room

For more critical listening rooms, utilizing the Meridian multi-room feature with local sources is an ideal, high quality solution 45. An ADA, Control4, Crestron or RTI could still control the Meridian, but the signals would bypass their preamp, sending direct digital signals to the speakers for superior sound. Want the entire system to be at the Meridian quality? Meridian offers their proprietary "speakerlink" that sends their digital information throughout your home on shielded CAT5e wiring.
