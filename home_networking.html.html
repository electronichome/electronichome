It’s important for homes to be easily adaptable to technological changes. Electronic Home can connect all of these products so that bandwidth problems and security concerns are properly solved and managed.

We provide superior networks that keep families connected and protected. The network can be partitioned into several Virtual Local Area Networks (VLANs) to separate the home office, family computers, home automation systems, and guest access. This keeps all private information secure from both the outside world and other users in your own home.

Our unified home network features a personal “to every client” air traffic controller. It dynamically modifies the wireless channels to keep your network running at peak performance. With permission, we will have secure, remote wireless access to monitor performance and update configuration and firmware without having to schedule an on-site service call.
